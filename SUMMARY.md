**Rust (Hemileia Vastatrix) and Leaf Miner (Leucoptera Coffeella) in Coffee Crop (Coffea Arabica)** is a dataset for an object detection task. It is used in the agricultural industry. 

The dataset consists of 542 images with 3487 labeled objects belonging to 2 different classes including *rust* and *leaf miner*. There are no pre-defined <i>train/val/test</i> splits in the dataset. The dataset was released in 2020 by the SÃ£o Paulo State Faculty of Technology, Brazil.
